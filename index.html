The End Is Near

Ah that old addage, the end is near. Why do people insist that some kind of end is just arond the corner?

Does that really even add up to anyone or anything? Seriously.

If the end were to be coming tomorrow, would you be ready for it?

So many thoughts come to mind...and yet I cannot even focus on one.
