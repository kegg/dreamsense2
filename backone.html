Be Kind To Others

Yesterday it was the plague, the death to millions. Today it's the coronavirus.

During all of this, just remember one simple thing.

Be kind to others.

It's a simple thing to remember isn't it? Be kind to your fellow men. Jesus taught this to people during his day. Love one another. Is it really that difficult to help people in time of need? I dare say it isn't.

Go forward with faith, your faith shall make you whole.
