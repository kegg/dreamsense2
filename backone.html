Life Is To Be Lived

Sometimes people don't understand that live is actually meant for living.

You're actually supposed to go out and explore things, see the world. Live to your fullest and try your best to actually live. If you don't try and live, what are you doing?

Living is a breath of fresh air. You are able to do so much when you're striving to see the good in people, the good in yourself, the good in everything out there. Sure there are things that aren't good for anyone, and those things should be avoided.

They say live like your'll die the next day. That's so true. Live like tomorrow is your last, you never know when the experiences will come again.
